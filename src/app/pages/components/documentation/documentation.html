Official guide for using and managing the EGFB Leaders Dashboard.

Overview

The EGFB Leaders Dashboard is a centralized platform for managing volunteer activities, tracking attendance through QR scanning, and generating real-time insights for coordinators and administrators.

Getting Started

  1. Log in using your assigned credentials.
  2. Open the Dashboard overview page.
  3. Select an activity before scanning volunteers.

QR Scanning

Each volunteer is assigned a unique QR code linked to their profile.

  • Scans register attendance instantly
  • Duplicate scans are automatically prevented
  • All data is synced in real time
Ensure camera permissions are enabled on your device.

Volunteers Management

This section allows coordinators to:

  • View all registered volunteers
  • Track participation history
  • Assign volunteer roles

Reports & Graphs

Reports provide visual insights into:

  • Total attendance per activity
  • Volunteer engagement over time

Roles & Permissions

Role Permissions
Admin Full access to all system features
Leaders Manage activities and scan attendance
Volunteer Fill the volunteer form only

Frequently Asked Questions

Check camera permissions and ensure the QR code is clearly visible.
